Covid-19 Impact on Bank Stocks

In this project, I wanted to explore the impact of Covid-19 on some banks and consulting companies. I looked at the Bank of America, Wells Fargo, Citigroup, Goldman Sachs, JP Morgan, Morgan Stanley, and Blackrock stocks from the beginning of the year to 08/11/2020.

I use pandas and numpy to calculate basic statistical data such as correlation, and percent change overtime. I use seaborn to create visualizations, for example the plot on the left represents JP Morgan's change in returns overtime.

The plot on the right represents Blackrock's 30 day average as compared to it's closing price over this time period.

I then use plotly to create interactive graphs. The plot below is Blackrock's candle stick graph of its daily opening, closing, high and low prices between 2020/01/01 and 2020/04/01.
